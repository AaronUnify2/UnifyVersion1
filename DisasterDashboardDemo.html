Weather Context — Southern Downs

The region experiences two distinct hazards. Thunderstorms and hail are often very localised — a severe storm may affect one town while nearby areas stay dry. Sustained regional rainfall across the Granite Belt causes flooding in the Condamine catchment. These are typically separate events. For flood information see the Flood tab.

Ember Zone Alert System

Watchtowers identifies properties within the projected ember throw zone of active incidents based on real-time wind direction and speed. Residents in the ember zone can be notified before fire arrives, allowing time to take protective action and reduce property damage and fire spread.

Condamine River Road

The 14 river crossings on Condamine River Road are only recommended in a 4WD. Mountain slopes channel water into the Condamine rapidly — do not attempt crossings if rain is forecast. An electric sign at the base of the crossing indicates closure. Check the flood cameras on the Disaster Dashboard map for recent pictures of crossing conditions.
