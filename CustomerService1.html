üìä How This Works (Decision Tree Model)

üéØ Structure Overview

4 Service Categories: The initial split groups all 16 call types into logical clusters, so you immediately know which area to focus on.

Decision Diamonds: Each diamond narrows options. Instead of reading a "wall of text," you answer a simple question (e.g., "Barking ‚Äì New or Ongoing?") and the path becomes clear.

Actionable References: Every node includes a specific Eli search term, form name, or contact number. No dead ends.

üìå Using Eli References

Every decision node includes a specific "Eli: 'Topic Name'" reference. This is your exact search term in the internal knowledge base.

No ambiguity. If it says "Eli: 'XYZ'", that's the document. Not a general search.
